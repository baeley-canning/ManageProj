About Us - Empowering Your Digital Growth

With a deep understanding of digital marketing, we specialize in driving growth for small to medium-sized businesses, transforming their online presence into measurable success.

Our Journey: From In-House to Empowering Small Businesses

Our story began with managing social media for some of New Zealand’s largest cleaning companies. Over time, we discovered our true passion for digital advertising and realized the immense impact we could have on small to medium-sized businesses. We thrive on helping these businesses establish an online presence that not only stands out but converts.

What started as a focus on social media management has grown into a comprehensive suite of services that includes Google Ads, social media strategy, Google Profile optimization, and more. We leverage data-driven insights to ensure that every campaign is optimized for results, helping our clients thrive in today’s fast-paced digital world.

Our Mission

At Manage Advertising, we believe in giving small to medium-sized businesses the tools they need to succeed online. Our mission is to make digital marketing accessible, effective, and result-oriented. Whether it's creating engaging social media content, managing Google Ads campaigns, or optimizing Google My Business profiles, we’re here to elevate your business to the next level.
